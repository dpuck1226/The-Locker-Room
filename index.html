About Us

The Locker Room is Kalamazoo’s newest Broncos‑themed sports bar, built for college students and anyone who lives for game day. Located downtown near the brand‑new arena, we’re the perfect spot to meet up before the puck drops, celebrate after a big win, or grab late‑night food with friends.

Our theme is simple: Western Michigan pride, sports energy, and a welcoming college‑town atmosphere. With TVs everywhere, real bar food, and prices made for students, The Locker Room is designed to be your go‑to hangout.

Whether you're here for wings, burgers, drinks, or just the vibe, one thing’s for sure — We major in gameday.
