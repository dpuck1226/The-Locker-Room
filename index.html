The Locker Room

Welcome to The Locker Room—Kalamazoo’s home for bold flavors, big games, and Bronco pride! Inspired by the spirit of Western Michigan University, we serve up classic eats, signature drinks, and a lively atmosphere where every guest is part of the team. Whether you’re here for a game, a night out, or just great food and company, you’ll always find a seat in The Locker Room.
